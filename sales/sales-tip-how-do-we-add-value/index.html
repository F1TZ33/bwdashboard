Sales Tip – How We Add Value

Language choices that protect trust, reduce rework, and lift perceived value.

Tip 1

Phrases That Undermine Confidence

Even when the information is correct:

  • ❌ “I’m not really sure…”
  • ❌ “I think this is right…”
  • ❌ “I don’t know much about these…”
  • ❌ “I’ve never seen one of these before…”
  • Why to avoid: They hand control to the customer and invite pressure or dismissal.
  • Say instead: ✔ “Let’s check this properly.” ✔ “We’ll confirm the details.” ✔ “I want to make sure this matches.”
Tip 2

Phrases That Invite Guessing

And future returns:

  • ❌ “It should be fine.”
  • ❌ “That’ll probably work.”
  • ❌ “People usually use this.”
  • ❌ “This is close enough.”
  • Why to avoid: Casual language creates casual decisions.
  • Say instead: ✔ “This matches the dimensions.” ✔ “This suits that application.” ✔ “This is the correct type for that setup.”
Tip 3

Phrases That Challenge the Customer

These create resistance and ego conflict:

  • ❌ “That’s wrong.”
  • ❌ “You can’t use that.”
  • ❌ “Who told you that?”
  • ❌ “That’s not how it works.”
  • Say instead: ✔ “That term gets used a few different ways.” ✔ “Let’s line it up with what you’ve got.” ✔ “That works in some cases — not this one.”
Tip 4

Phrases That Over-Promise Expertise

Risky and unnecessary:

  • ❌ “I know exactly what you need.”
  • ❌ “Trust me.”
  • ❌ “I’ve been doing this for years.”
  • ❌ “This is definitely the best option.”
  • Say instead: ✔ “Based on what you’ve described…” ✔ “This is the correct match for that setup.” ✔ “This aligns with the application.”
Tip 5

Phrases That Sound Defensive

These weaken authority instantly:

  • ❌ “That’s just what the book says.”
  • ❌ “That’s how we always do it.”
  • ❌ “I’m just following policy.”
  • ❌ “Don’t blame me if…”
  • Say instead: ✔ “That’s the spec for this application.” ✔ “That’s what prevents repeat failures.” ✔ “That’s the safest option here.”
Tip 6

Phrases That Invite Rushing

Pressure leads to mistakes:

  • ❌ “If you’re in a hurry…”
  • ❌ “We can just grab something quick.”
  • ❌ “This should get you by.”
  • Say instead: ✔ “Let’s take 30 seconds and confirm it.” ✔ “The right part saves time later.” ✔ “Accuracy matters here.”
Tip 7

Phrases That Give Away Control

Especially with confident customers:

  • ❌ “What do you want to do?”
  • ❌ “Up to you.”
  • ❌ “You tell me.”
  • ❌ “Whatever you think.”
  • Say instead: ✔ “Here’s what I recommend.” ✔ “This is the correct path for that issue.” ✔ “This option avoids the common problems.”
Tip 8

Phrases That Devalue Measurement

Dangerous in bearings, seals, belts, and chain:

  • ❌ “That looks about right.”
  • ❌ “They’re usually all the same.”
  • ❌ “It should fit.”
  • Say instead: ✔ “Let’s confirm the measurements.” ✔ “Once we measure it, it’s clear.” ✔ “Size will tell us.”
Tip 9

Phrases That Kill Trust After the Sale

Used when issues arise:

  • ❌ “No one else has complained.”
  • ❌ “That’s never happened before.”
  • ❌ “It must’ve been installed wrong.”
  • Say instead: ✔ “Let’s work through what happened.” ✔ “We’ll figure out why it failed.” ✔ “Let’s check the application again.”
Tip 10

The One Phrase to Never Use

Under any circumstances:

  • ❌ “That’s the only one we have.”
  • Why: It forces the customer into a bad decision.
  • Say instead: ✔ “This is what we stock — let’s make sure it’s suitable.” ✔ “We can look at alternatives if needed.”
